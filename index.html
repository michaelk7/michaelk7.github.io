OSF Diabetic Ketoacidosis Project

Machine learning
The goal is to predict Diabetic Ketoacidosis (DKA) given medical testing data using machine learning. DKA affects people with type 1 diabetes when the body breaks down fat too quickly and there is not enough insulin. The liver turns these fats into acids called ketones. Given prior patient data, we will build a model that achieves the highest level of accuracy in predicting DKA. To test the accuracy of our model, we are aiming to achieve the lowest root mean square error.
